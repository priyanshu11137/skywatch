Camera Calibration

Step 1 — Print Checkerboard

Click below to download a checkerboard pattern. Print it on A4 paper at 100% scale (no fit-to-page). Measure the actual printed square size with a ruler and enter it below.

Place the board flat on the ground at the center point of your camera setup. This becomes your coordinate origin. The board's long edge points "north" (+Y).

Step 2 — Upload Checkerboard Photos

From each phone's final mounted position, take a photo that includes the full checkerboard. The phone should be pointing roughly toward the sky area you want to monitor.
